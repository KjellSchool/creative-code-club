---
title: "Prusa Research Partners with Open Source Robotics Lab"
date: 2025-02-12
---

Prusa Research, the renowned 3D printer manufacturer known for its high-quality desktop printers, has announced a partnership with the Open Source Robotics Lab, a leader in community-driven robotics projects.  

As part of the collaboration, Prusa will integrate several open-source robotics components into its printer ecosystem, enabling users to experiment with robotic add-ons and automation features. Early prototypes have already been showcased on the [Prusa website](https://www.prusa3d.com/).
